# Robotic-Dog--Tinkercad-3D-Model
 This project demonstrates basic 3D modeling concepts using simple geometric shapes such as boxes, cylinders, spheres, and wedges.


Tools Used:

Tinkercad (Autodesk); Used for creating and assembling the 3D shapes.

Model Dimensions (Properties):

Body:

- Length: 60 mm  

- Width: 30 mm  

- Height: 25 mm  



Head:

- Length: 25 mm  

- Width: 25 mm  

- Height: 25 mm  



Camera Eye (Cylinder):

- Radius: 4 mm  

- Height: 4 mm  



Antennas (Cylinder):

- Radius: 2 mm  

- Height: 15 mm  



Legs:

Upper Leg (Cylinder)

- Radius: 3 mm  

- Height: 20 mm  



Joint (Sphere)

- Radius: 4 mm  



Lower Leg (Cylinder)

- Radius: 3 mm  

- Height: 20 mm  



Tail:

- Length: 15 mm  

- Width: 8 mm  

- Height: 10 mm
